About ChurnIQ

An intelligent platform for B2B and B2C companies to understand, predict, and prevent customer churn using machine learning and AI-powered insights.

Platform Overview

ChurnIQ is a full-cycle churn intelligence platform designed for analysts, product managers, and executives. By uploading your customer CSV data, the platform automatically computes churn metrics, segments customers by risk, and surfaces actionable insights through interactive visualizations.

The built-in AI Advisor uses large language models to answer complex questions about your data — from identifying root causes to generating month-by-month retention roadmaps.

Version History
